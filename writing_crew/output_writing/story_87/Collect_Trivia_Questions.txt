Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story centered around Remy's extraordinary journey in "Ratatouille":

1. **Question:** Who was the author of 'Pygmalion'?  
   **Answer:** George Bernard Shaw.

2. **Question:** What is the minimum age of someone running for the office of President of the United States?  
   **Answer:** 35 years old.

3. **Question:** England won the 1966 World Cup beating Germany 4-2. Hurst scored 3 of England's goals; who scored the other?  
   **Answer:** Geoff Hurst also scored the last goal, and Martin Peters scored one of the goals for England.

4. **Question:** From which 1960s sci-fi movie did the 1980s pop sensation Duran Duran take their name from a character from the movie?  
   **Answer:** Barbarella.

5. **Question:** The Red Sea is connected to the Indian Ocean by which gulf?  
   **Answer:** Gulf of Aden.

These trivia questions can be integrated into a narrative that reflects the themes of passion, individuality, and culinary creativity. Each question serves as an engaging learning opportunity, weaving knowledge into the storytelling experience while celebrating the joy of discovery, much like Remy's journey in pursuing his dreams against all odds.
