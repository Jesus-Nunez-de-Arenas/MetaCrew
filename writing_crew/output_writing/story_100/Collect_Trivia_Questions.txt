Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the story:

1. **What is the name of the steam locomotive which set the world speed record in July 1938 by reaching 126 mph on the East Coast Mainline in England?**  
   **Answer:** Mallard.

2. **Singer and musician Bono called which venue ‘Rock and roll’s great cathedral’?**  
   **Answer:** The Apollo Theatre.

3. **Which 5th/6th Century Buddhist monk is described in Chinese texts as ‘The Blue-Eyed Barbarian’?**  
   **Answer:** Bodhidharma.

4. **Which year saw the only hijacking of a British commercial flight?**  
   **Answer:** 1971.

5. **What was the name of the Cunard ocean liner which was launched on 20th September 1906?**  
   **Answer:** RMS Mauretania.

These trivia questions and answers can enrich the protagonist's journey, allowing for memorable encounters that emphasize the themes of history, identity, and connection, enhancing the overall narrative of love and acceptance.
