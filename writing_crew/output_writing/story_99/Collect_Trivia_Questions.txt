Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that will be seamlessly integrated into the story of "The Legend of Zelda," reflecting its themes of heroism, courage, and the quest for self-discovery:

1. **Question:** Which British monarch famously said ‘I don’t wish to open windows into men’s souls’?  
   **Answer:** Queen Elizabeth II.

2. **Question:** Which British singer played Jareth the Goblin King in the 1986 film ‘Labyrinth’?  
   **Answer:** David Bowie.

3. **Question:** The 1987 film ‘Cry Freedom’ is a biographical drama about which South African civil rights leader?  
   **Answer:** Steve Biko.

4. **Question:** Which British actress played Valerie in the 1997 film ‘Nil By Mouth’?  
   **Answer:** Kathy Burke.

5. **Question:** What was the name of the Brazilian player who won the Women’s Singles Finals at Wimbledon in 1959, 1960, and 1964?  
   **Answer:** Maria Bueno.

These trivia questions enhance the narrative of "The Legend of Zelda" by providing engaging moments for characters to reflect on the nature of bravery, the quest for identity, and the legacy of hope and knowledge, bringing depth to their journeys through Hyrule.
