The central theme of Cinderella revolves around the transformation and empowerment that comes from inner strength and kindness, juxtaposed with the struggles of overcoming adversity and oppression. The story illustrates how virtue and resilience can lead to personal growth and ultimate triumph over one's circumstances. Through Cinderella's journey from mistreatment to self-discovery, the narrative highlights the importance of hope, compassion, and the belief that deserving love and happiness can prevail against all odds. In the end, it conveys the message that true beauty and worth stem from one's character, not social status, and that integrity and kindness can create a brighter future.
