Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the story of "The Lorax," illustrating themes of environmental stewardship and the complexities of industrialization:

1. **Question:** Who became Israel's head of state in 1993?  
   **Answer:** Shimon Peres.

2. **Question:** In WWII, who was the head of the Nazi party's security service?  
   **Answer:** Heinrich Himmler.

3. **Question:** Which state on the Gulf of Mexico is nearest the end of the alphabet?  
   **Answer:** Texas.

4. **Question:** Truax Field international airport is in which US state?  
   **Answer:** Wisconsin.

5. **Question:** In the 1904 Olympics, how many of the 23 track and field titles were won by Americans?  
   **Answer:** 21.

These trivia questions not only provide insight into various historical moments and geographical facts but can also be woven into the narrative to encourage discussions on the importance of protecting the environment and the history of our societal choices. Each question acts as a pivot point to reflect on how our past informs our environmental responsibilities today, echoing the core messages of caring for the Earth central to "The Lorax."
