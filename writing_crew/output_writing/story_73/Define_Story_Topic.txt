The central theme of the story "Trolls" revolves around the power of music, the importance of self-acceptance, and the strength found in embracing individuality. Through their colorful and vibrant adventures, the characters—particularly the trolls—navigate a world where personal expression is often challenged by societal norms. The narrative emphasizes that true happiness and connection emerge when individuals openly share their unique gifts and bond through shared experiences. The story explores the struggle between conformity and the freedom to be oneself, highlighting the transformative power of love, friendship, and creativity. Moreover, it underscores that while external threats may arise, the greatest strength lies within the community's ability to unite, uplift each other, and celebrate their differences, ultimately conveying that together, they can overcome adversity and find joy in the harmony of their diverse voices.
