Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story reflecting themes of transformation, interconnectedness, and empathy, much like the narrative of "Brother Bear":

1. **Question:** In UK currency, how many sides does a twenty pence piece have?  
   **Answer:** Seven sides.

2. **Question:** Which British artist’s works include ‘The First Real Target’?  
   **Answer:** Richard Hamilton.

3. **Question:** Which King of England sold Dunkirk to Louis XIV of France?  
   **Answer:** King Charles II.

4. **Question:** What was the middle name of US actor James Dean?  
   **Answer:** Byron.

5. **Question:** Which French author wrote the fairy tales ‘Little Red Riding Hood’, ‘Sleeping Beauty’, and ‘Puss in Boots’?  
   **Answer:** Charles Perrault.

These trivia questions can be woven into Kenai's journey in "Brother Bear," allowing for moments of learning and introspection. Each question serves to deepen the narrative, connecting the audience to the themes of compassion, understanding, and the richness of both nature and human experience as Kenai transforms through his unique adventure.
