1. **Who is Arthur Wellesley better known as?**  
   Answer: The Duke of Wellington.

2. **Which 1986 Peter Weir movie and based on a Paul Theroux novel starred Harrison Ford, Helen Mirren and River Phoenix?**  
   Answer: The Mosquito Coast.

3. **What is the name of CS Lewis' 7 book fantasy?**  
   Answer: The Chronicles of Narnia.

4. **Name the TV series about FBI agent Don Eppes and his mathematical genius brother Charlie.**  
   Answer: Numb3rs.

5. **What is the famous opening line from J.M. Barrie's "Peter Pan"?**  
   Answer: "All children except one grow up." 

These trivia questions and answers can be woven into a story about self-discovery and the importance of friendship, just like in the adventures of the DigiDestined. Each question can serve as a catalyst for character growth and exploration of various themes throughout the narrative.
