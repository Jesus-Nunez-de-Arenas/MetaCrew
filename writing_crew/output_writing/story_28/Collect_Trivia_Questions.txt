1. **Who said, "To err is human but it feels divine?"**  
   **Answer:** Alexander Pope.

2. **Which singer had a big 60s No 1 with "Roses Are Red"?**  
   **Answer:** Bobby Vinton.

3. **What are the international registration letters of a vehicle from Iceland?**  
   **Answer:** IS.

4. **Where was the American venue of the Live Aid concert?**  
   **Answer:** John F. Kennedy Stadium in Philadelphia.

5. **Which was the longest moon landing?**  
   **Answer:** Apollo 17. 

As the story of Mario unfolds against the backdrop of New York, these trivia questions serve as a reflection of the diverse experiences, capturing the essence of life's unpredictability. Each question weaves into the narrative, enriching the tapestry of his journey and inviting readers to explore the depths of knowledge intersecting with the artistic expression of music and memories.
