Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story that reflects the themes of family, identity, and the quest for connection, similar to "Parent Trap":

1. **Question:** Who holds a trumpet on the album cover of Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band by The Beatles?  
   **Answer:** Billy Shears.

2. **Question:** Which song begins with the line "The taxman's taken all my dough"?  
   **Answer:** "Taxman" by The Beatles.

3. **Question:** The Kray twins were convicted of the murder of George Cornell in the East End of London in 1966. What was the name of the pub in which he was shot?  
   **Answer:** The Blind Beggar.

4. **Question:** Who composed the musical theme for the Pink Panther?  
   **Answer:** Henry Mancini.

5. **Question:** Which actress married Dennis Quaid on Valentine's Day in 1991?  
   **Answer:** Meg Ryan.

These trivia questions not only spark curiosity but also enrich the storytelling by providing intriguing facts that mirror the journey of self-discovery and the importance of family connections, aligning perfectly with the themes portrayed in "Parent Trap."
