   **Answer:** Scott Maslen. 

2. **Question:** What type of animal was Boris, the subject of a song by The Who?  
   **Answer:** An elephant.

3. **Question:** Which brand of beer does Homer Simpson drink regularly?  
   **Answer:** Duff Beer.

4. **Question:** In which film was the song White Christmas first sung?  
   **Answer:** Holiday Inn.

5. **Question:** Which Pop Idol had a number one hit in 2004 called All This Time?  
   **Answer:** Oasis.

These trivia questions not only intrigue but also enhance the storytelling, inviting readers to reflect on the various elements of life that mirror the journey of Buttercup and Westley.
