Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be integrated into a heartwarming story about friendship and community, inspired by the colorful world of "Strawberry Shortcake":

1. **Question:** Which film features the Dawes Tomes Mousley Grubbs Fidelity Fiduciary Bank?  
   **Answer:** Mary Poppins.

2. **Question:** What is Robin Williams' character called in Good Morning Vietnam?  
   **Answer:** Adrian Cronauer.

3. **Question:** Who plays Cruella de Vil in the 1996 live-action version of 101 Dalmatians?  
   **Answer:** Glenn Close.

4. **Question:** Marilyn Monroe starred in The Seven Year Itch. Who directed it?  
   **Answer:** Billy Wilder.

5. **Question:** On Red Nose Day 2001, who won Celebrity Big Brother?  
   **Answer:** Jack Dee.

These trivia questions will add an engaging layer to the narrative, enriching the themes of collaboration and sharing woven throughout the adventures of Strawberry Shortcake and her friends. Each question serves as a delightful pause in the storytelling, inviting the audience to connect with movie moments and personalities that resonate with the sweet spirit of friendship in Berry Bitty City.
