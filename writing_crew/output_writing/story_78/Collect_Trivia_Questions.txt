Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can seamlessly integrate into the narrative of "Madagascar," enhancing themes of friendship, adventure, and self-discovery:

1. **Question:** What was the first name of the character played by John Travolta in Saturday Night Fever?  
   **Answer:** Tony.

2. **Question:** In which 1969 movie did Michael Caine appear with his brother Stanley?  
   **Answer:** The Italian Job.

3. **Question:** In 1969, a man was stabbed to death by a member of the Hell's Angels at a Rolling Stones concert. Where was the concert held?  
   **Answer:** Altamont Speedway.

4. **Question:** How long does it take, approximately, for light to travel from the Sun to the Earth?  
   **Answer:** About 8 minutes.

5. **Question:** Anellini pasta is what type of shape?  
   **Answer:** Ring-shaped.

These trivia questions can be woven into the journey of Alex, Marty, Melman, and Gloria as they navigate their new world in Madagascar, emphasizing their growth, the strength of their friendships, and the excitement of discovering the infinite wonders around them, much like the intriguing facts presented in these questions.
