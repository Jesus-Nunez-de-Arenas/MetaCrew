Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the narrative of "The Land Before Time," highlighting themes of resilience, friendship, and the journey through adversity:

1. **Question:** Which film sees Clint Eastwood, Tommy Lee Jones, James Garner, and Donald Sutherland as retired astronauts?  
   **Answer:** Space Cowboys.

2. **Question:** In a standard deck of cards, how many Kings have a moustache?  
   **Answer:** Two Kings (the King of Hearts and the King of Clubs).

3. **Question:** According to the film Gremlins, if you are looking after a Gremlin you should not feed him after midnight, not get him wet and not expose him to what?  
   **Answer:** Light.

4. **Question:** The Canary Islands are named after what animal?  
   **Answer:** Dogs (the name comes from the Latin term "Canariae insulae," meaning "Islands of Dogs").

5. **Question:** What was the surname of the woman who was the inspiration behind the Rolling Stones song Angie?  
   **Answer:** Bowie (the song was inspired by David Bowie's ex-wife, Angela Barnett, whose maiden name is Bowie).

These trivia questions can be woven into the narrative of "The Land Before Time" to create engaging moments where characters encounter challenges that echo the questions. By integrating these facts, the story not only entertains but also educates, emphasizing the importance of curiosity and knowledge on their path to the Great Valley.
