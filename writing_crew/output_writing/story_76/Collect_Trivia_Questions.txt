   **Answer:** "Ivan Basso."

2. **In the Commonwealth Games in Delhi, which British athlete won the men's 200m gold medal?**  
   **Answer:** "Daniel Talbot."

3. **Which singer picked up three Ivor Novello awards in May, including Songwriter of the Year for her hit single, The Fear?**  
   **Answer:** "Lily Allen."

4. **Which famous woman in history had a cottage in Shottery in Warwickshire?**  
   **Answer:** "Anne Hathaway."

5. **Who sang the theme song for the TV show 'Rawhide'?**  
   **Answer:** "Frankie Laine."

These trivia questions not only provide engaging facts for the audience but also complement the narrative of "Despicable Me" by enriching the storytelling experience with historical and cultural references, thus enhancing the themes of connection, growth, and the importance of relationships in one's life journey.
