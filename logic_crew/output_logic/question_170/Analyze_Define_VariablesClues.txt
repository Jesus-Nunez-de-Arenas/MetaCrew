   - Clue 1: The coffee drinker lives in the second house.
   - Clue 2: The person who is eating apples lives directly left of the person who only drinks water.
   - Clue 3: The person who is eating mangos does not live in the first house.
   - Clue 4: The person who is eating mangos does not live in the third house.
   - Clue 5: The person who is eating apples lives somewhere to the left of the tea drinker.
   - Clue 6: The person who is eating kiwis does not live in the third house. 

These variables work together to create a logical framework for analyzing the placement of individuals, their drinks, and their fruits within the given houses.
