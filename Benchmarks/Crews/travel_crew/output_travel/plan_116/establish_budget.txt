### Overall Budget Breakdown for 7-day Travel Plan (March 22-28, 2022) for 5 People from Provo, UT to California

#### 1. Transportation
- **Provo to San Diego**
  - **Travel Option:** Self-Driving
  - **Distance:** 1,137 km
  - **Duration:** 10 hours 13 minutes
  - **Cost:** $56
- **San Diego to Santa Ana**
  - **Travel Option:** Self-Driving
  - **Distance:** 144 km
  - **Duration:** 1 hour 26 minutes
  - **Cost:** $7
- **Santa Ana to Bakersfield**
  - **Travel Option:** Self-Driving
  - **Distance:** 231 km
  - **Duration:** 2 hours 31 minutes
  - **Cost:** $11
- **Bakersfield to Provo**
  - **Travel Option:** Self-Driving
  - **Distance:** 1,063 km
  - **Duration:** 9 hours 34 minutes
  - **Cost:** $53

**Total Transportation Cost:**
- $56 + $7 + $11 + $53 = **$127**

#### 2. Accommodation 
- **San Diego (4 nights)**:  
  - **Accommodation:** Luxury 4BR Home, Spacious & Central to Trains
  - **Cost per Night:** $392 
  - **Total Cost:** $392 * 4 = **$1,568**

- **Santa Ana (2 nights)**:  
  - **Accommodation:** Sun-filled Artist Home 1BR in convenient L.I.C
  - **Cost per Night:** $1,065 
  - **Total Cost:** $1,065 * 2 = **$2,130**

- **Bakersfield (1 night)**:  
  - **Accommodation:** DaDukes Dreams
  - **Cost:** **$491**

**Total Accommodation Cost:**
- $1,568 + $2,130 + $491 = **$4,189**

#### 3. Food
- **Food Budget (per day for 5 people)**: $50 per meal * 3 meals * 7 days = **$1,050**

#### 4. Activities
- **San Diego Attractions:**
  - Cabrillo National Monument: **Free**
  - La Jolla Shores Park: **Free**
  - SeaWorld San Diego: **$90 per person** * 5 people = **$450**
  - San Diego Zoo: **$60 per person** * 5 people = **$300**
  
  **Total Activity Cost in San Diego:** $0 + $0 + $450 + $300 = **$750**
  
- **Santa Ana Attractions:**
  - Bowers Museum: **$15 per person** * 5 people = **$75**
  - Santa Ana Zoo: **$10 per person** * 5 people = **$50**
  - Discovery Science Center: **$15 per person** * 5 people = **$75**
  
  **Total Activity Cost in Santa Ana:** $75 + $50 + $75 = **$200**

- **Bakersfield Attractions:**
  - Kern County Museum: **$10 per person** * 5 people = **$50**
  - Buena Vista Museum: **$10 per person** * 5 people = **$50**
  - California Living Museum: **$10 per person** * 5 people = **$50**
  
  **Total Activity Cost in Bakersfield:** $50 + $50 + $50 = **$150**

**Total Activity Cost:**
- San Diego + Santa Ana + Bakersfield = $750 + $200 + $150 = **$1,100**

### Total Estimated Vacation Cost
- Transportation: **$127**
- Accommodation: **$4,189**
- Food: **$1,050**
- Activities: **$1,100**

**Total Cost:**  
$127 + $4,189 + $1,050 + $1,100 = **$6,466**

**Budget:** $14,900  
**Remaining Budget:** $14,900 - $6,466 = **$8,434** 

This plan provides a detailed overview of the expenses for transportation, accommodation, food, and activities for a 7-day vacation for a group of 5, ensuring a memorable experience while staying well within the budget.
