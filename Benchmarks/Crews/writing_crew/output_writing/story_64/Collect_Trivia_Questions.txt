Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story about the "Power Rangers":  

1. **What public school did the Duke of Edinburgh and Prince Charles attend?**  
   **Answer:** Gordonstoun School  

2. **If it was Phil Mickelson in 2006, Zach Johnson in 2007, and Trevor Immelman in 2008, who was it in 2009?**  
   **Answer:** Angel Cabrera  

3. **What is the name of the Salvador Dali painting that shows clocks oozing over a landscape?**  
   **Answer:** The Persistence of Memory  

4. **Which well known couple live at Bagshot Park in Surrey?**  
   **Answer:** The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge  

5. **Which notorious murderer lived at 10 Rillington Place?**  
   **Answer:** John Christie  

These trivia questions can be creatively woven into the narrative of the "Power Rangers," emphasizing themes of teamwork, identity, and moral choices while providing engaging moments for the audience.
