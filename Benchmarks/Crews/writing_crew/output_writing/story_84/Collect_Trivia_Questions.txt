1. What was the capital of the United States between 1785 and 1790?  
   - Answer: New York City
   
2. Which fictional girls' boarding school was created by the cartoonist Ronald Searle?  
   - Answer: St. Trinian's School
   
3. Who played the character Mr. Chips in the 2002 TV adaptation of Goodbye Mr. Chips?  
   - Answer: Martin Clunes
   
4. Rarely is the question asked: Is our children learning?  
   - Answer: George W. Bush (this was a quote from a speech he made)
   
5. Are you going to come quietly, or do I have to use earplugs?  
   - Answer: This line is attributed to the character Sergeant Major in the film "The Long Good Friday."  

These trivia questions and answers can be seamlessly integrated into the rich narrative of "The Princess and the Frog," highlighting Tiana's journey and aspirations. They serve as a representation of her knowledge, curiosity, and the whimsical nature of her adventure in pursuing her dreams.
