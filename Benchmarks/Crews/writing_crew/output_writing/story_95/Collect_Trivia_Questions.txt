Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the story focusing on the exploration of identity, belonging, and the intersection of wildness and civilization:

1. **Question:** Although he never won an Oscar for best director, one of Hitchcock's films did win an Oscar for best picture. What was the film?  
   **Answer:** "Rebecca."

2. **Question:** Which cartoon character had the maiden name ‘Slaghoople’?  
   **Answer:** Wilma Flintstone.

3. **Question:** Which ‘A’ is a calculating frame with sliding balls on wires?  
   **Answer:** An abacus.

4. **Question:** Which ‘A’ means in the open air?  
   **Answer:** Al fresco.

5. **Question:** Which vegetable was the first to be canned?  
   **Answer:** Peas.

These trivia questions can serve as stimulating conversational elements within the narrative of "Tarzan," enriching the themes of identity and belonging while engaging readers or listeners on multiple levels.
