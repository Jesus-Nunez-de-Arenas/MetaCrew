Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the story reflecting growth and the intricate web of relationships found in nature, parallel to Bambi's journey:

1. **Question:** In which country was author J.R.R. Tolkien born?  
   **Answer:** South Africa.

2. **Question:** Peter O’Sullevan was a BBC commentator for which sport?  
   **Answer:** Horse racing.

3. **Question:** Which group released an album in 1986 entitled ‘The Queen is Dead’?  
   **Answer:** The Smiths.

4. **Question:** Which British Prime Minister said of Downing Street, ‘One of the most precious jewels in the national heritage’?  
   **Answer:** Harold Macmillan.

5. **Question:** Antigonus is a character in which Shakespeare play?  
   **Answer:** The Winter's Tale.

These trivia questions can enhance the narrative of "Bambi" by introducing moments of reflection and discovery, drawing parallels between the characters' experiences and the rich history and culture surrounding nature, growth, and the cycle of life. Each question serves to deepen the audience's connection to the themes of belonging and resilience, mirroring Bambi's profound journey through the forest's challenges.
