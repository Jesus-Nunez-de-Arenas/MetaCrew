1. **Question:** Macbeth belonged to which royal house or dynasty?  
   **Answer:** The House of Duncan.

2. **Question:** Was James II known as: James The Bald, Fiery Face, or The Red King?  
   **Answer:** Fiery Face.

3. **Question:** In Harry Potter And The Goblet Of Fire, which dragon did Harry have to defeat?  
   **Answer:** The Hungarian Horntail.

4. **Question:** What is the full name of the train robber played by Phil Collins in a 1988 movie?  
   **Answer:** The full name is Bobby "Boris" Pickett.

5. **Question:** Tony Hadley was the lead singer with which 1980s new romantic band?  
   **Answer:** Spandau Ballet.
