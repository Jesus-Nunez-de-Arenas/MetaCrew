Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story:  

1. **Question:** Which wife of Henry VIII had already married twice before she became queen, and married for a fourth time after Henry's death?  
   **Answer:** Catherine Parr

2. **Question:** In which country is the castle that gives the Habsburg dynasty its name?  
   **Answer:** Austria

3. **Question:** In which parliament has the UK 87 seats for members who are elected for a five-year term?  
   **Answer:** The House of Commons

4. **Question:** What is the more common name of the "simple plurality" system of voting?  
   **Answer:** First-past-the-post

5. **Question:** What was the nickname of the French singer Edith Piaf?  
   **Answer:** The Little Sparrow  

These questions and answers can enrich the narrative by adding layers of historical and cultural depth, making the characters' explorations through their emotional lives even more compelling.
