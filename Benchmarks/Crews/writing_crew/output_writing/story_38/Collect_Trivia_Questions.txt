Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the story of The Flintstones, highlighting themes of community and timeless values:

1. **Question:** What was the first British football team to win the European Cup?  
   **Answer:** Celtic FC.

2. **Question:** How many pieces are there in a standard set of dominoes?  
   **Answer:** 28 pieces.

3. **Question:** What were Benjy and Laska, which were sent into space in 1958?  
   **Answer:** They were dogs.

4. **Question:** On the London Underground, which is the only line to connect at some point with every other line on the system?  
   **Answer:** The Circle line.

5. **Question:** Thomas Edison, who invented the lightbulb, was afraid of the dark?  
   **Answer:** True.

These trivia questions will add an engaging layer to the narrative, allowing for interactive moments that connect with the universal themes of family, community, and experience reflected in The Flintstones. By weaving these facts into the story, it enhances audience participation and encourages reflection on shared values across different contexts.
