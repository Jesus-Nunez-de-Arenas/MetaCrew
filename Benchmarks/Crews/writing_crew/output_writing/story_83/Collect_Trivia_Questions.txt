1. **Question**: Following that year's April Budget, Alderman Sir Cuthbert Ackroyd, who later became Lord Mayor of London, bought the first what on 1 November 1956?  
   **Answer**: The first plastic banknotes.  

2. **Question**: Produced between 1970 and 1974, what is a Bond Bug?  
   **Answer**: A small, three-wheeled car designed for leisure and fun.  

3. **Question**: What was Jacques Cousteau's mobile oceanographic laboratory?  
   **Answer**: The Calypso.  

4. **Question**: What is the name of the helicopter used to transport the President of the United States?  
   **Answer**: Marine One.  

5. **Question**: Which queen consort of England is associated with Hever Castle in Kent?  
   **Answer**: Anne Boleyn.
