Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that will be seamlessly integrated into the story of "NeverEnding Story," reflecting its themes of imagination, courage, and the power of belief:

1. **Question:** Which Channel 4 TV show's presenters included Mark Lamarr, Amanda De Cadenet, and Terry Christian?  
   **Answer:** The Word.

2. **Question:** Who won the Ladies singles title at Wimbledon in 2004?  
   **Answer:** Maria Sharapova.

3. **Question:** In which year did St George die?  
   **Answer:** It is traditionally believed to be around AD 303.

4. **Question:** What are the names of the Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse?  
   **Answer:** Conquest (or Pestilence), War, Famine, and Death.

5. **Question:** Who is the Vice-President for George W Bush?  
   **Answer:** Dick Cheney.

These trivia questions can be woven into the narrative to enhance the storytelling, allowing characters to draw parallels between their magical adventures in Fantastica and the real-world knowledge, further emphasizing the significance of learning and imagination throughout the tale.
