   **Answer:** Magpie.

2. **Question:** What type of animal was Robin Hood in the Disney cartoon film 'Robin Hood'?  
   **Answer:** A fox.

3. **Question:** Who provided the voice for Bob The Builder on his number one hit "Can We Fix It?"  
   **Answer:** Neil Morrissey.

4. **Question:** Which actor received 4 million dollars for his 10 minutes on screen in the film Superman?  
   **Answer:** Marlon Brando.

5. **Question:** In cricket, what is the score of 111 known as?  
   **Answer:** A "sandwich."

These trivia questions will enhance the narrative by introducing intriguing facts that parallel the themes of exploration and learning.
