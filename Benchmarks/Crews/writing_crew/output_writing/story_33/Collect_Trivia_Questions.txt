Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the storytelling context of "The Lego Movie":

1. **Question:** What raw material is used for making glass?  
   **Answer:** Silica (sand).

2. **Question:** What name is given to the change of state from liquid to gas?  
   **Answer:** Evaporation.

3. **Question:** Which branch of science has four fundamental principles: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division?  
   **Answer:** Mathematics.

4. **Question:** What is the fastest animal on two legs?  
   **Answer:** The ostrich.

5. **Question:** Mosses are classified as belonging to which part of the plant kingdom?  
   **Answer:** Bryophytes.

These trivia questions not only enhance dialogue but also encourage engagement with audiences, as they reflect the film's themes of creativity and individual expression within playful constructs.
