1. **Question:** Which act won the Eurovision Song Contest for the United Kingdom singing "Love Shine A Light"?  
   **Answer:** Katrina and the Waves.

2. **Question:** Which team beat American Samoa 31-0 in a qualifying match for the 2002 World Cup, but still failed to qualify?  
   **Answer:** Australia.

3. **Question:** What was the name of Roland Rat's gerbil friend?  
   **Answer:** Reggie.

4. **Question:** Which animal has the longest gestation period at around 22 months?  
   **Answer:** African elephant.

5. **Question:** Who dated actress Elizabeth Hurley between 2000 and 2001 and denied he was the father of her son born in 2002 before a DNA test proved otherwise?  
   **Answer:** Steve Bing.

These trivia questions can be woven into the narrative of a story reflecting the essence of childhood and friendship, providing moments of engaging learning while enhancing the overall storytelling experience.
