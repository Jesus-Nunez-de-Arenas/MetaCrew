Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that will seamlessly incorporate into the story about "The Peanuts Movie":

1. **Trivia Question:** Which rodent rears its young, called kittens, in a nest called a drey?  
   **Correct Answer:** The squirrel.

2. **Trivia Question:** Which mammal constructs a lodge in which to store food, rear young, and pass the winter?  
   **Correct Answer:** The beaver.

3. **Trivia Question:** What is the generic word for plants that grow in water or water-logged conditions?  
   **Correct Answer:** Aquatic plants.

4. **Trivia Question:** When the egg is released from the ovary, what is left behind?  
   **Correct Answer:** The corpus luteum.

5. **Trivia Question:** What is the scientific name for the heart's pacemaker?  
   **Correct Answer:** Sinoatrial node.

Incorporating these trivia elements into the story will add an engaging educational layer to the themes of perseverance and self-discovery present in "The Peanuts Movie."
