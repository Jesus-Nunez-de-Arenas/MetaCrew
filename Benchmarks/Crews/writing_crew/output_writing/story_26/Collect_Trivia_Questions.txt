Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be integrated into a story about adventure and self-discovery:

1. **Question:** Which home of champagne in France was also where the German High Command surrendered in WWII?  
   **Answer:** Reims.

2. **Question:** Which was the first European city this century to open an underground railway system?  
   **Answer:** Naples, Italy.

3. **Question:** Who first drew Mickey Mouse when Disney first supplied the voice?  
   **Answer:** Ub Iwerks.

4. **Question:** Which actress wrote the novel "The Last of the Really Great Whangdoodles"?  
   **Answer:** Julie Andrews.

5. **Question:** Who was the star of the film based on the record "Harper Valley PTA" by Jeannie C. Riley?  
   **Answer:** Barbara Eden.

These trivia questions will enrich the narrative by introducing fascinating facts that resonate with the themes of identity and exploration within a whimsical storyline. Each question adds depth and engages the audience, allowing for a playful and curious approach to the unfolding adventure.
