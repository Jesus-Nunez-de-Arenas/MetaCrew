1. **In which events did Gert Fredriksson win six Olympic gold medals?**  
   **Answer:** Canoeing.

2. **How many of his 45 races did Mike Hawthorn, a former Formula One motor racing world champion, win?**  
   **Answer:** 3 races.

3. **On the Internet what is Spam?**  
   **Answer:** Unwanted or unsolicited messages, typically sent in bulk.

4. **In which year was the Juilliard School founded in New York?**  
   **Answer:** 1905.

5. **Who wrote My Life and Loves in 1926, originally banned in the US?**  
   **Answer:** Frank Harris.
