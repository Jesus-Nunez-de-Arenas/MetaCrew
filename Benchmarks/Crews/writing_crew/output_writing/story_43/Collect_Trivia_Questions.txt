Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story that highlights the adventures and lessons learned in "Rugrats":

1. **Question:** Who wrote the novels About A Boy, How To Be Good and High Fidelity?  
   **Answer:** Nick Hornby.

2. **Question:** With which game would you use a squidger, a circular disc between 25mm and 51 mm?  
   **Answer:** Croquet.

3. **Question:** The melody for which famous song was written by sisters and school teachers Patty and Mildred Hill in Kentucky in 1893?  
   **Answer:** "Happy Birthday to You."

4. **Question:** What is the most common pub name in the UK?  
   **Answer:** The Red Lion.

5. **Question:** What is the name of Kevin the teenager's friend played by Kathy Burke in a series of sketches with Harry Enfield?  
   **Answer:** Julie.

These trivia questions will enrich the narrative of "Rugrats," providing engaging and humorous moments that reflect the creativity and curiosity of childhood. As the characters embark on their imaginative adventures, these questions serve as delightful interludes that celebrate the joy of discovery and the importance of friendship, enhancing the overall storytelling experience.
