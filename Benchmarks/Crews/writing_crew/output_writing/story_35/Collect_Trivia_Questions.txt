Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into the storytelling context of "The Emoji Movie":

1. **Question:** Which hormone helps control ovulation?  
   **Answer:** Luteinizing hormone (LH).

2. **Question:** What is the second most common gas in the atmosphere?  
   **Answer:** Oxygen (O2).

3. **Question:** What is the term for nutrient enrichment of lakes?  
   **Answer:** Eutrophication.

4. **Question:** Five-legged creatures have damaged which 1250 mile long wonder of the world?  
   **Answer:** The Great Barrier Reef.

5. **Question:** What is the approximate circumference of the earth?  
   **Answer:** About 24,901 miles (40,075 kilometers).

These trivia questions not only enhance the narrative but also invite audiences to reflect on the importance of knowledge, diversity, and understanding in a world full of varying identities and experiences, much like the characters in "The Emoji Movie."
