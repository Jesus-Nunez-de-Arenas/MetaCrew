Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story centered around the values of community, cooperation, and resilience, as exemplified by the Smurfs:

1. **Question:** What was advertised with Eva Herzigova using the slogan "Hello Boys"?  
   **Answer:** Wonderbra.

2. **Question:** What number in Bingo is sometimes referred to as "Heinz varieties"?  
   **Answer:** 57.

3. **Question:** As of March 2001, who holds the record for scoring the most league goals for the soccer team Arsenal?  
   **Answer:** Thierry Henry.

4. **Question:** Who was the American President when the Berlin Wall was constructed?  
   **Answer:** John F. Kennedy.

5. **Question:** What is the minimum number of darts a person needs to throw to complete a leg from 501?  
   **Answer:** 3 darts.

These trivia questions can enhance the narrative, bringing a lively and interactive element that resonates with the themes of the Smurfs and their adventures, encouraging cooperation and teamwork among the audience and characters alike. Each question sparks curiosity and fosters engagement, further embodying the spirit of friendship and community in the Smurf village.
