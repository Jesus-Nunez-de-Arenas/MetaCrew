Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a narrative focused on adventure and self-discovery:

1. **Question:** What color golf balls are used on the golf course in Antarctica?  
   **Answer:** Yellow.

2. **Question:** Throughout the 80s and 90s Phil Collins recorded on which record label?  
   **Answer:** Atlantic Records.

3. **Question:** On which label did Chuck Berry record in the 1950s and 1960s?  
   **Answer:** Chess Records.

4. **Question:** What was the name of Michael Jackson's second wife?  
   **Answer:** Lisa Marie Presley.

5. **Question:** Which US city was named after a British Prime Minister?  
   **Answer:** Winston-Salem (named in part after Winston Churchill).

These trivia questions will enrich the narrative by introducing fascinating facts that resonate with the themes of identity and exploration within a whimsical storyline. Each question adds depth and engages the audience, allowing for a playful and curious approach to the unfolding adventure, echoing Mowgli's journey of self-discovery in "The Jungle Book."
