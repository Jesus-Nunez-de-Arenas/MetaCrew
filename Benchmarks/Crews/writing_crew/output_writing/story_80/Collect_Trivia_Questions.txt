Here is a list of five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be incorporated into the story:

1. **Question:** What kind of creature is Firenze?  
   **Answer:** A centaur. 

2. **Question:** What building in Scotland is said to house the Holy Grail and features prominently at the end of The Da Vinci Code?  
   **Answer:** Rosslyn Chapel.

3. **Question:** In which sitcom did Penelope Wilton play the wife of Richard Briers?  
   **Answer:** Ever Decreasing Circles.

4. **Question:** Who plays the role of Bubble in Absolutely Fabulous?  
   **Answer:** Jane Horrocks.

5. **Question:** Which cockney private detective, co-created by Terry Venables, was portrayed on TV by Nicholas Ball?  
   **Answer:** Hazell.

These trivia questions can serve as engaging points within the narrative, allowing characters to interact with each other's knowledge and histories, further emphasizing the themes of friendship and collaboration as they navigate their adventures.
