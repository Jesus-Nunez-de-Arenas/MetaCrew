Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly woven into a narrative about exploration and the importance of imagination:

1. **Question:** Which orchestral conductor was married to one of the subjects of the film Hillary and Jackie?  
   **Answer:** Daniel Barenboim.

2. **Question:** In what year was the first Tarzan novel published?  
   **Answer:** 1912.

3. **Question:** In which country were motorized ambulances first used?  
   **Answer:** France.

4. **Question:** Which Syrian city is said to be the oldest continually inhabited city in the world?  
   **Answer:** Damascus.

5. **Question:** What is the name of the cathedral in Red Square, Moscow?  
   **Answer:** Saint Basil's Cathedral.

These trivia questions can enhance the narrative by introducing captivating historical and cultural elements that resonate with themes of discovery and belief. As the characters embark on their journey, each question serves to spark curiosity, encouraging readers or listeners to reflect on the wonders of the world and the magic of knowledge, aligning beautifully with the overarching themes of "The Polar Express."
