Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that can be seamlessly integrated into a story about empowerment, friendship, and the adventures of the "Powerpuff Girls":

1. **Question:** What are the only two musical notes which have no flats?  
   **Answer:** B and C.

2. **Question:** From which country do French fries originate?  
   **Answer:** Belgium.

3. **Question:** For what novel did J. K. Rowling win the 1999 Whitbread Children's book of the year award?  
   **Answer:** Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone.

4. **Question:** What is the longest word that can be typed using only the top row of letters on a typewriter?  
   **Answer:** Typewriter.

5. **Question:** What are the names of Donald Duck's three nephews?  
   **Answer:** Huey, Dewey, and Louie.

These trivia questions not only enhance the narrative by providing fun facts but also resonate with the themes of challenge and determination that the "Powerpuff Girls" embody. Each question invites the audience to reflect on their knowledge while celebrating the uniqueness of each character and their collective journey towards heroism and friendship.
