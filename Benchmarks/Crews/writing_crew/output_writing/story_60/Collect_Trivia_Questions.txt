Here are five trivia questions along with their correct answers that will be seamlessly integrated into the colorful world of "Rainbow Brite," emphasizing themes of friendship, optimism, and overcoming darkness:

1. **Question:** How many runs did Donald Bradman score in his last ever test match innings?  
   **Answer:** 52 runs.

2. **Question:** Nicknamed the Cannibal, who is the only rider to have won all three jerseys in the same year in the Tour de France?  
   **Answer:** Eddy Merckx.

3. **Question:** Whose arch-nemesis is the Red Skull?  
   **Answer:** Captain America.

4. **Question:** First appearing on TV in 1967, which detective solved all of his crimes sitting down?  
   **Answer:** Columbo.

5. **Question:** How many times was Wyatt Earp shot?  
   **Answer:** He was shot five times.

These trivia questions, when woven into the story of "Rainbow Brite," will provide moments of reflection and discovery, illustrating how knowledge and the power of friendship combine to illuminate the journey ahead, reinforcing the message that each individual can contribute to a brighter world through learning and unity.
